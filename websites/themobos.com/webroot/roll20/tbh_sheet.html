Warrior Cleric Thief Conjurer

WARRIOR

  • Weapons & Armor: Any and All
  • Hit Dice: 1d10
  • Once per hour, whilst in combat, a Warrior can regain d8 lost HP.
  • As part of their action a Warrior can make 1 attack per level.
  • If a Warrior fails a STR or DEX test and would be dealt damage from an attack, they can opt to sunder (destroy) their shield - if they have one equipped - and ignore the damage.

CLERIC

  • Weapons & Armor: All Blunt Weapons, Gambeson, Leather, Chain Mail, All Shields
  • Hit Dice: 1d8
  • Roll with Advantage when testing CON to avoid damage from poison or being paralyzed.
  • A Cleric can spend an action to banish all Nearby undead by testing their WIS and adding the creature's HD to the roll.
  • Spells starting at level 2

THIEF

  • Weapons & Armor: All Swords, All Bows, Daggers, Gambeson, Leather, Small Shields
  • Hit Dice: 1d6
  • Roll with Advantage when testing DEX to avoid damage or effects from traps and magical devices.
  • Rolls with Advantage when attacking from behind and deals 2d6 / 2d4 + the Thief's level damage.
  • Rolls with Advantage when performing delicate tasks, climbing, hearing sounds, moving silently, understanding written languages and opening locks.

CONJURER

  • Weapons & Armor: 1-handed Sword and Staff
  • Hit Dice: 1d4
  • Conjurers start with a large spellbook containing a total of 1d4+2 spells from the Level 1 and 2 Arcane Spell lists. (see bottom of page)

JESTER

  • Weapons & Armor: Club, Dagger, Sling, Staff, All Swords, Leather
  • Hit Dice: 1d6
  • Once per hour, whilst in combat, a Jester rolls with Advantage on an ATTRIBUTE test against any spell, trap, or effect that would impact them.
  • Enemies roll with Disadvantage when attacking a Jester from Nearby with a ranged weapon (thrown or bow/crossbow). When a Close or Nearby enemy rolls a 20 targeting a Jester with a ranged weapon attack, the Jester automatically catches the weapon and hurls it back at the enemy, dealing 1d6 damage.
  • Rolls with Advantage when climbing, juggling, mimicking voices, picking pockets and telling jokes.

HUMAN

  • 1x day, automatically succeed on an ATTRIBUTE test to resist a trap, spell, or effect.

DWARF

  • 1x day, roll with Advantage on an INT test to resist spell or magic
  • 1x day, roll with Advantage on a CON test to resist poison
  • Detect slopes, new construction, sliding rooms, and depth underground
  • Can see out to a distance of Far-Away in the dark

ELF

  • 1x day, roll with Advantage on an INT test to resist a sleep spell
  • 1x day, roll with Advantage on a CHA check to resist a charming effect
  • Rolls with Advantage when searching for secret doors
  • Can see out to a distance of Far-Away in the dark

HALFLING

  • 1x day, a Halfling who succeeds on their attack roll with a bow or sling, may deal critical damage.
  • Rolls with Advantage when testing DEX for Initiative.
You can carry a number of items equal to your STR. Carrying more means all tests have Disadvantage
